💰 Broker Balances

      
    

    

      Live cash + collateral, dono brokers se. Total Margin (cash+collateral)
      isi se LIVE strategies ka "Global Max Loss %" cap calculate hota hai — neeche 1️⃣ Strategy Day Cap
      section dekho. "Live orders bhejo" decide karta hai naye LIVE entries kis
      broker pe jaayenge (`risk_gate.default_broker()` — har strategy isi ko padhta hai, jab tak kisi ka apna
      explicit `broker` override na ho).
    

    

  


  

    
🚦 Rate Limit Monitor

    

      Dhan API rate-limit (DH-904) events, last 15 minutes — har event "kaun (strategy:symbol)"
      aur "kya (throttle = wait karke mil gaya / 429 = Dhan ne reject kiya)" ke saath tagged hai.
      Auto-refresh 5s.

⚠️ Max-Loss Protection

    

      Do alag-alag, independent guardrails — dono 3 level pe kaam karte hain:
      Global default (yahan set karo) → Per-Strategy override
      (table neeche, blank = global use hoga) → Per-Trade manual (kisi bhi open
      position ke ⚙️ modal se, kabhi bhi badal sakte ho — woh hamesha sabse upar priority leta hai).
    


    

      
1️⃣ Strategy Day Cap

      

        Pura din ke liye — strategy ka cumulative P&L (saari open+closed legs milake) itna ₹/% loss
        cross kare to poori strategy ke liye naye entries ruk jaate hain aur
        open positions square-off ho jaati hain.
      

      

        
        
        
        
      

      

        PAPER strategies: ₹ cap use hota hai (jo upar 💰 Broker Balances
        card me "dono diye ho to jo pehle hit ho wahi chalega"). LIVE
        strategies (uska jo broker — Dhan ya Zerodha — woh trade kar rahi hai): % cap ab us broker ke
        REAL Total Margin (cash+collateral) ka % hota hai, ₹ cap ko replace karke — har baar fresh
        balance se calculate hota hai, kabhi stale nahi.
      

    


    

      
🎯 Daily Profit Target

      

        Jab aaj ka combined realized P&L is target ko cross kare →
        saari open positions immediately square-off + us din ke liye
        koi bhi nayi entry nahi. Blank = off.
      

      

        
        
      

      

        Max-loss ki tarah hi kaam karta hai — par profit cross hone pe trigger hota hai. Dono independent hain.
      

    


    

      
2️⃣ Per-Trade Default SL

      

        Har individual naye position (kisi bhi instrument) ke khulte hi yeh ₹
        stop-loss automatically lag jaata hai — sirf wahi ek leg square-off hoti hai, baaki strategy/instrument
        pe asar nahi. Hit hone ke baad usi/kisi bhi instrument me nayi entry phir bhi le sakte hain.
      

      

        
        
      

    


    

      
3️⃣ Account-Level Trailing Profit Lock 🔒

      

        Jab aaj ka total P&L (realized + unrealized) apne peak se neeche aaye →
        saari positions auto-squareoff. Sirf tab fire hota hai jab peak ≥ ₹1 ho.
        
• 1 position open → Fixed ₹ use hoga  |  2+ positions open → % of peak use hoga (jo bhi set hai)
        
Blank = off. Dono blank = trailing lock off.

💰 Capital Allocation

    

      Global capital — sab strategies milake itne se zyada deploy nahi hoga. Jo signal capital
      na hone ki wajah se nahi li gayi, woh Orders & P&L me "🚫 Capital" tag ke saath dikhegi (real order nahi jaata).
      Margin Multiplier: option-SELL karne me premium se zyada margin block hota hai
      (real broker number nahi, ek estimate) — multiplier se SELL legs ka "capital used" notional se zyada dikhega
      (e.g. 5 = qty×premium×5). BUY legs (premium pay karna) is se affect nahi hote. Default 1 (off).
    

    

      
      
      
      
      
      
      
      
      
    

  


  

    
🧪 Shadow-Live Testing (DANGEROUS — OFF by default)

    

      ON karne par har PAPER trade ke saath ek REAL Dhan order bhi parallel me fire hoga (same price/qty) —
      sirf fill/reject compare karne ke liye (slippage check). Paper P&L is se kabhi affect nahi hota.
      Sirf tab ON karo jab Dhan account balance zero/near-zero ho (warna real order fill ho sakta hai!).
      Test khatam hote hi OFF kar dena.
    

    

      
      
      
    

  


  

    
🛡️ Auto-Hedge (naked option SELL → auto BUY further OTM)

    

      Jab koi strategy option SELL karti hai (abhi sirf range_trader/ARS_CHAIN_V1), uske turant baad ek
      hedge BUY same option type, further OTM, automatically jaata hai. Min Strikes =
      kam se kam itne strikes ATM/sold-leg se door. Max Premium ₹ (optional) = agar set hai,
      hedge Min Strikes se aur door chalta rehta hai jab tak us strike ka premium is ₹ se kam/equal na mil jaye
      (NIFTY jaise instruments me sasta hedge dhoondhne ke liye) — jo bhi door wala strike mile (Min Strikes ya
      Max Premium), wahi use hota hai. Dono khali = hedge OFF.
    

    

      
      
      
      
      
    

  


  

    
📊 Live Liquidity Filter (any 2-of-3, ON by default)

    

      Static stock-whitelist se zyada flexible — har entry se pehle us EXACT option contract ka
      live market depth check hota hai: Spread% ≤ 1.5 ((ask-bid)/ltp×100),
      Volume lots ≥ 500 (≥50 before 9:45 AM, volume naturally kam hota hai
      session shuru mein), OI lots ≥ 300. Teeno mein se kam se kam 2 pass
      karna kaafi hai — ek dimension thoda weak ho to bhi trade ho sakta hai, isse range zyada strict
      whitelist se badi hai aur naye liquid stocks bhi automatically mil jaate hain bina manual list
      update kiye. Live data na mile (feed cold/down) to FAIL-OPEN hota hai (block nahi karta) — yeh
      illiquid-contract guard hai, P&L-correctness guard nahi.
    

    

      
      
      
    

  


  

    
Per-Strategy Override

    

      Khali rakho to global default lagega. Naye banaye gaye strategy bhi yahan automatically aa jayengi.
